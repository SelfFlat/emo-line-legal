This Privacy Policy describes what data the EMO LINE mobile application (the “App”) processes, for what purpose and on what legal basis.

In short

EMO LINE is an emotion diary. We deliberately designed it to work locally:

  • Your notes, mood ratings, emotions and attachments are stored only on your device. We do not send them to our servers, do not sync them, and have no access to them.
  • You have no account with us: the App can be used without registration or sign-in.
  • The only data that leaves your device is anonymous usage statistics (Google/Firebase analytics), which does not contain the text of your notes or your emotions.

The flip side of local storage: if your device is lost, damaged or reset, your records cannot be recovered, because no one but you holds a copy. Use the export/backup feature as a safeguard.

1What data is processed

1.1. Diary content — on your device only

The text of your notes, mood ratings (emoji), selected emotions and attached files (photos, audio) are stored in a local database on your device and are not transmitted anywhere else. We, as the operator, have no access to this data and do not store it on any servers.

1.2. Usage analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase)

The App uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how it is used (for example, number of launches, session length, stability). It collects automatic, anonymized data, including:

  • technical device information (model, OS version, language, region);
  • the app instance identifier (Analytics App Instance ID) and the device advertising identifier (if available);
  • usage events (for example, first open, session start);
  • approximate location (country/region level, derived from the IP address).

This data is processed by Google. The text of your notes, your emotions and mood ratings are not sent to analytics.

3What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not show ads and do not embed advertising networks.
  • We do not collect or analyze the content of your notes on our servers.
  • We do not use diary content to train models or for profiling.

4Sharing data with third parties

Anonymized analytics and (in the future) push infrastructure are processed by our cloud service provider:

  • Google LLC / Firebase — analytics and push notifications. This processing is governed by Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics data may be processed on Google's servers outside your country.

5Data retention and deletion

  • Diary content is stored on the device until you delete it (deleting an individual record, clearing the App's data, or uninstalling the App). Once deleted, it cannot be recovered.
  • Export/backup lets you save your data to a file at your discretion; you are responsible for storing and protecting the exported file.
  • Analytics data is retained by Google for a period defined by the Firebase settings ([state the retention period]).
